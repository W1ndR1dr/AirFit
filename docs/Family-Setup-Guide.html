AirFit Coach

Your Personal AI Fitness Coach

1

Install TestFlight

Open the App Store on your iPhone and search for "TestFlight". Download and install the free app from Apple.

2

Accept the Invitation

Check your email for a TestFlight invitation from Brian. Tap "View in TestFlight" to open the app in TestFlight.

3

Install AirFit Coach

In TestFlight, tap "Install" next to AirFit Coach. Wait for the download to complete, then tap "Open".

4

Connect to the Server

When the app opens, it will ask you to connect to the AI server. Scan the QR code below or enter the server address manually.

Scan This QR Code

Point your iPhone camera at this code during app setup

Manual entry: http://192.168.86.50:8080
5

Allow Health Access

The app will ask to connect to Apple Health. Tap "Allow All" so your AI coach can see your workouts, sleep, and activity data.

6

Start Chatting!

You're all set! Start a conversation with your AI fitness coach. Ask about nutrition, workouts, recovery, or anything fitness-related.

Quick Tips

  • Same Wi-Fi - Make sure you're connected to the home Wi-Fi network to reach the server
  • Track Meals - Just tell the AI what you ate, and it'll log your nutrition automatically
  • Voice Input - Tap the microphone icon to speak instead of type
  • Updates - TestFlight will notify you when new versions are available
